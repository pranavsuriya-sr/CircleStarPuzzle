Circle Shade Star Puzzle

Puzzle Rules:

1. You need to place 10 discs on the star points

2. Jumps must start from a yellow (unshaded) circle

3. Each jump moves in a straight line, skipping exactly one point

4. After each jump, place a disc on the landing point

5. Points can be revisited but jumps can only start from shaded circles

6. The goal is to place all 10 discs on the star points
